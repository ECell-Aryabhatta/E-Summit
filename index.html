About
E-Summit 2021

The E-Summit is an annual 2-day formal event, comprising competitions, seminars and workshops, where leaders from various fields come and share their experience and ex. The events provide an avenue for students to exhibit their talent, creativity and innovation about the rapidly developing entrepreneurial world through interesting & mind blowing competitions. The focus of the summit is to network with industry experts, inculcate a thought process towards entrepreneurship in the minds of future entrepreneurs in a professional and an efficient manner.

E-Summit 2018.

The annual two-day, E-Summit 2018, was a successful formal event conducted in the month of April,2018. With the amphitheatre packed to the brim, the first day kicked off with Speaker sessions by the stalwarts of the industry and ended with a Business Model Canvas Workshop in coordination with Mr Puneet Raman from Prowisdom Growth. Day two of the summit witnessed a stellar B-Plan Competition. The Event came to a positive and successful end with one of the speakers taking equity and adding three B-Plan Entries to his portfolio.

E-Summit 2019.

E summit, a 2-day event conducted in the month of April 2019, witnessed interactive expert sessions with stalwarts from the industry, followed by workshops and Business Plan competition. Amidst the overwhelming excitement of the audience, Mr Vivek Kapoor, the Founder of Dineout, became the first speaker, followed by Mr Aditya Arora, the CEO of FAAD Networks and Mr Raman Bhatia the MD of Servotech Power Systems Limited. The Vice president of Paytm, Saurabh Jain concluded day-1 with an interactive session. Dr Manoj Sinha, the Principal of Aryabhatta College commenced Day-2 with heartwarming sessions with Mr Kumar Sharadindu, the MD & CEO of SBI Pension Funds. The Business-Plan competition, which was organized on Day 2 was a huge success in which students were given the opportunity to present their future business proposals to the members of the jury, and the winning team was awarded cash prize and certificates. One of the remarkable milestones that E-cell achieved on E-summit was launching its official website, Smartphone App, and its first start-up.

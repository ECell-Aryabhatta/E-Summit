E-Summit 2018.

The annual two-day, E-Summit 2018, was a successful formal event conducted in the month of April. The event kicked off with Speaker sessions by the stalwarts of the industry and ended with a Business Model Canvas Workshop in coordination with Mr Puneet Raman. Day two of the summit witnessed a stellar B-Plan Competition. The Event came to a positive and successful end with one of the speakers taking equity and adding three B-Plan Entries to his portfolio.

E-Summit 2019.

E summit, a 2-day event was conducted in April 2019, witnessed interactive expert sessions with stalwarts from the industry, followed by workshops and Business Plan competition. The speakers were Mr Vivek Kapoor, Mr Aditya Arora, Mr Raman Bhatia, Saurabh Jain and Mr Kumar Sharadindu. The Business-Plan competition was a huge success where students were deemed to present their future business proposals to the members of the jury and the winning team was awarded cash prize and certificates.
